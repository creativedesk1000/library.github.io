Our Mission

At Comsats Wah Library, our mission is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment for all our users. We aim to foster a culture of reading, learning, and discovery through our diverse collection of resources and services.

Our History

Established in 2000, Comsats Wah Library has grown from a small collection of books to a comprehensive resource hub. Over the years, we have adapted to the changing needs of our community, incorporating digital resources and modern technology into our services.

Facilities and Services

Our library offers a range of facilities, including:

  • Study rooms for individual and group study
  • Access to computers and free Wi-Fi
  • A diverse collection of books, journals, and digital resources
  • Regular workshops and events for the community

We invite you to become a member of our library and explore the various resources we offer. Join us in our mission to promote literacy and lifelong learning in our community.
